Problem

  • Consolidation in the livestock industry has reduced competition and options for farmers.
  • Farmers have been forced from family to factory models of production, and open to contracted models of commerce.
  • Rural economies, animal welfare, human health, and the environment have all been impacted by these changes.
  • Public activism is needed to rebalance the ag economy and build a sustainable, productive future.

Meta-Problem

  • Public policy results are too often buried in PDF files and rarely-read glossy reports.
  • It has been too hard to tie analysis to activism and positive change.
