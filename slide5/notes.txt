Caption:
In the Iowa hog industry, farmers have been pressured to merge due to consolidation by meatpackers. As national market share of the top four hog packers rose over a 40% threshold, the rural economy in counties with the largest hog farms shrank.

Citation:
Factory Farm Nation: How American Turned Its Livestock Farms into Factories (2010). Food & Water Watch. http://www.foodandwaterwatch.org/reports/factory-farm-nation/
